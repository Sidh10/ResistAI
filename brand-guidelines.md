# Brand guidelines — ResistAI

## Identity
- App name: ResistAI
- Tagline: "Predict resistance. Prescribe with confidence."
- Tone: Clinical precision meets accessible clarity. Authoritative but never
  jargon-heavy. Speaks like a trusted senior colleague, not a research paper.

## Colors
- Primary: #C0392B (deep red — urgency, resistance, medicine)
- Secondary: #2E86AB (steel blue — data, trust, science)
- Background: #F7F9FC (cool off-white — clinical, clean)
- Text: #1A1A2E (near-black)
- Accent: #E8F4FD (light blue for highlights, success states, safe predictions)
- Warning: #F39C12 (amber — intermediate resistance)
- Danger: #E74C3C (bright red — confirmed resistance)
- Safe: #27AE60 (green — susceptible / recommended antibiotic)

## Typography
- Headings: Inter Bold
- Body: Inter Regular
- Data labels & metrics: Inter Medium (tabular nums)
- Code/SMILES strings: JetBrains Mono

## UI style
- Clinical dashboard aesthetic — data-dense but organized
- Rounded corners (8px), subtle card shadows
- Color-coded resistance status throughout (red/amber/green system)
- SHAP chart as a horizontal bar chart — most important feature at top
- Antibiotic recommendation displayed as a ranked list with confidence scores

## What to avoid
- No decorative gradients
- No abstract health illustrations or stock imagery
- No cluttered layouts — one prediction, one explanation, one recommendation per view
- Never show a prediction without its SHAP explanation alongside it
